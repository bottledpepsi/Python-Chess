## What is this?

Python Chess is a free, open source chess app with a clean board, smooth animations, and no ads or accounts. Play locally against a friend, or challenge a computer opponent with 10 difficulty levels, from beginner-friendly all the way up to genuinely tough.

Turn on live analysis to see how strong each move is, add a chess clock if you want timed games, and don't worry about losing your progress. Everything saves automatically as you go.

## Highlights

- **Two ways to play.** Pass-and-play with a friend, or take on the computer.
- **Adjustable difficulty.** 10 levels, from casual to challenging.
- **Optional analysis.** See move quality and top suggestions as you play.
- **Chess clock.** Add a time limit for a faster-paced game.
- **Games save automatically.** Close the app anytime and pick up later.
- **Export to PGN.** Save a finished game to share or review elsewhere.
- **Customizable look.** Board colors, arrow colors, and a colorblind-friendly theme.
- **Built for accessibility.** Full keyboard support and high-contrast text.
