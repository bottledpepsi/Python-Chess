b2 · Using the app

Settings & customization

Open Preferences from the main menu, or from the in-game menu without leaving your current game.

Board colors

Pick from four board themes:

  • Green
  • Blue
  • Red
  • Colorblind-friendly, a deuteranopia-friendly palette

Arrow colors

Arrows you draw on the board (right-click and drag) can use any of five colors: Blue, Yellow, Green, White, or Black.

Sound

Turn sound effects on or off entirely from Preferences.

Motion

If you'd rather cut down on animation, turn on reduced motion. It affects piece movement, board flipping, and the fade on the game-over screen.

Everything gets saved

Your preferences stick around between sessions, so you only need to set them up once.

Tip

You can open Preferences mid-game from the in-game menu. Choosing "Back" drops you right back into your game instead of the main menu.
