What is Python Chess?

Python Chess is a desktop chess app for people who just want to sit down and play. No account, no ads, no internet connection needed. Play a friend on the same computer, or take on a built-in computer opponent that goes from pretty relaxed all the way up to genuinely hard.

You can turn on analysis to see how good each move is while you play, add a clock to keep things moving, and not worry about losing your spot. The game saves itself as you go, so closing the app is never a problem.
